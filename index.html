Why Amazon?

Overview

  1. The stock is a short-term winner, too
    • In 2020, Amazon stock rose by 5.5% through March 31
  2. The pandemic should provide a big initial boost to Amazon’s e-commerce revenue
    • People buying from Amazon to limit their exposure to the virus
    • Spike in the purchases of office supplies as people are working from homes
    • Most people experienced the luxury of services from e-commerce therefore benefitting the shareholders of the stock
  3. Amazon Prime membership is growing briskly
    • The number of Prime Memberships have grown drastically over 3 years
    • Prime members are spending more than double the amount than non prime members, therefore helping the economy and the stock price’s rise
  4. U.S. online sales continue to take market share from brick-and-mortar sales
    • Americans are starting to prefer online shopping, making upto 11.4% of all retail sales in the U.S.
    • This number shows that there is a lot of potential for growth, as more and more local businesses are partnering up with Amazon for more sales
  5. Online shopping continues to grow in popularity around the world
    • Percent of retail sales worldwide also seems to be growing, and with access to technology across the world, Amazon’s services are preferred
  6. Its e-commerce business will never be dethroned
    • Amazon being the king of e-commerce business, there is no real competition to Amazon’s business scale therefore a safe investment for the future.
  7. Wide Range of services for everyday living
    • Amazon’s brands and wide range of services help in almost every day-to-day activities, becoming a part of many people’s lives
    • Amazon is conquering every other industry, including Cloud Technologies, Grocery, Entertainment, Security and many more
    • This assures safety for the investment and definitely creates a throne for itself in everyone’s Portfolios
